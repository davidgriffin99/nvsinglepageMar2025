What's different about Nicolas Ventures?

March 02, 2025 by David Griffin

Many charitable organizations will do job-training. Or they will volunteer at businesses big and small (Paid or unpaid). The big issue with this approach is it's difficult to find businesses that want to engage. Often the business owner or the senior VP has some connection to people with special needs and wants to assist...which is great. But when times get tough, these opportunities often fade away. Nicolas Ventures sort of turns this on its head. The idea is more along the lines of running a regular business and always trying to have people with special needs work there in some capacity whenever possible. Its's sort of built into the DNA.
